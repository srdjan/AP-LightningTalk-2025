The Problem: Over-Confidence Is Expensive 💸

"I've canceled your subscription. Have a great day! 👋"

Plot twist: That was a $10K annual contract 😱

Rule 1: No Cape, No Crime ✅

✅

Full Autonomy

When to let the agent run free:

• Clearly documented (FAQ answers)
• Low stakes (no money, no legal stuff)
• Easily reversible (no harm if wrong)
• If outside that scope → say "I don't know" and escalate

Example:
Customer: "What are your business hours?"
Agent: "We're open Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm EST!"

✓ Can't mess this up

Rule 2: Show Your Work 📝

📝

Propose & Confirm

When the agent should draft but ask:

• Medium stakes (some money, but reversible)
• Needs personalization
• Should be verified

Example:
Agent to You: "Customer qualifies for refund. I drafted this message—look good?"

✓ Agent did 90% of the work

Rule 3: Escalate Like a Pro 🚨

🚨

Escalate Immediately

When to call for backup:

• High stakes (money, legal, brand)
• Irreversible (can't undo)
• Emotionally charged (customer upset)
• Policy exceptions needed

Example:
"🚨 High-value customer ($45K) expressing frustration. Wants full cancellation. This needs YOU ASAP."

✓ Agent recognized it's in over its head

How to Actually Build This 🛠️

Before responding, ask yourself:

1. CONFIDENCE CHECK
- Am I 80%+ sure about this?
- If not → SAY SO

2. WHAT'S AT STAKE?
- Can this be undone?
- Money involved?

3. DECISION TREE
- Low stakes + High confidence = GO
- Medium = Draft & ask
- High = Escalate with context

Modern LLMs can follow these instructions! 🎉

A Real Prompt That Works 📋

You are a customer support agent. Before responding:

1. ASSESS YOUR CONFIDENCE (0-100)
- Do I have verified information about this?
- Am I certain about company policy here?

2. CHECK THE STAKES
- Money involved? (>$100 = medium, >$500 = high)
- Can this be reversed if wrong?
- Is customer showing frustration?

3. DECIDE & ACT
- If confidence >80% AND low stakes → Respond directly
- If confidence 60-80% OR medium stakes → Draft response, prefix with
"[DRAFT - Please review:] ..."
- If confidence <60% OR high stakes → Output:
"[ESCALATE] Reason: ... | Context: ... | Suggested action: ..."

Always explain your reasoning briefly in your response.
